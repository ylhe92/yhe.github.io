Brief Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Birmingham (UoB) working with Prof. Hongming Xu. I received the B.S. and M.E. degrees in the School of Energy and Power Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China, in 2014 and 2017. In June 2021, I would expect to receive a Ph.D. degree in automotive control from the University of Birmingham for my work on Evaluation and Optimization of Powertrain Operation and Driving Behaviour for Electrified and Automated Vehicles.

I am broadly interested in learning, optimization, and control of vehicle and transport systems.

I will be joining the Directorate for Energy, Transport, and Climate of Joint Research Centre (JRC, Ispra) in the European Commission as a Research Assistant in September 2020, working with Dr. Biagio Ciuffo.
