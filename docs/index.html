Brief Biography

I am a final year Ph.D. student in Automotive and Transportation Engineering at the University of Birmingham (UoB), UK. I received the B.S. and M.E. degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST, ranked 9th in China), in 2014 and 2017, respectively. In June 2021, I would expect to receive a Ph.D. degree in automotive control from the University of Birmingham for my work on Evaluation and Optimization of Powertrain Operation and Driving Behaviour for Electrified and Automated Vehicles. I am broadly interested in learning, optimization, and control of vehicle and transport systems.

I will be joining the European Commission - Joint Research Centre (JRC) as a technology expert to perform automotive and traffic software integration from Sep. 2020 to July 2021.
